To use this wallpaper on an iPhone connected to a PC:

  • Right click on the picture and select "Save Image As"
  • Save the image in your "My Pictures" folder
  • In iTunes, click on the iPhone icon in the Devices category in the left-hand menu
  • In the tabbed iPhone menu, select "Photos" and check the "Sync photos from: My Pictures" box
  • Once synched, select the image on your iPhone and select "Use as Wallpaper"

To use this wallpaper on an iPhone connected to a MAC:

  • Right-click or Control-click on the picture and choose "Add Image to iPhoto Library."
  • In iTunes, click on the iPhone icon in the Devices category in the left-hand menu
  • In the tabbed iPhone menu, select "Photos" and check the "Sync photos from: iPhoto" box
  • Once synched, select the image on your iPhone and select "Use as Wallpaper"
