Download Instructions:

  1. Right-click on the Fairy icon and pick “Save Image As ...”
  2. Save the image to your Desktop.
  3. Open your Instant Messenger application.
  4. Select the Buddy Icon chooser.
  5. Choose “Browse” to go to the Desktop where you saved the icon in Step 1 and select the image you want to use for your buddy icon.
  6. Click OK.
